What We Do

Four wings, one mission.

Food Distribution

Daily meals for underprivileged families and street animals — because no one should go to sleep hungry.

Education Access

School supplies, uniforms and learning support so a child's future isn't decided by their family's income.

Menstrual Health

Awareness drives and free sanitary pads, breaking the silence and stigma around periods.

Clothing Drives

Warm clothes and essentials collected and distributed directly to families who need them most.

Student Volunteers

Run entirely by school and college students, proving young people can lead real change today.

Community Drives

On-ground campaigns across UP, bringing hands-on relief directly to the people who need it.

Our Story

Born in crisis, built to stay.

NayePankh started during the toughest months of the Covid lockdown, when basic necessities became out of reach for thousands of families. What began as a small relief effort grew into one of India's largest student-led organizations — a name that means "new wings," because that's exactly what we wanted to give the people we serve.
